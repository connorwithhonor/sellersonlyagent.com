Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

SellersOnlyAgent.com (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is operated by Connor MacIvor in connection with real estate services. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you use this website or contact us.

Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and property address when you submit a contact or strategy-review form
  • Any messages or notes you include in forms (e.g., timeline, goals, questions)
  • Information you send by email or other communication in connection with a potential or actual listing or transaction

We may also automatically collect certain technical data when you visit the site, such as IP address, browser type, device type, and pages visited. We may use cookies or similar technologies to improve site function and analytics.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and schedule or conduct seller strategy calls
  • Provide real estate services and communicate about listings, transactions, and related matters
  • Send follow-up communications you have requested or that are reasonably related to your inquiry
  • Improve our website, services, and marketing (including aggregated or anonymized analytics)
  • Comply with legal or regulatory obligations

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers that help us operate the site or business (e.g., hosting, email, CRM, scheduling tools), subject to confidentiality obligations

Data Security

We use reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

You may opt out of marketing emails by using the unsubscribe link in those emails or by contacting us. You can disable or limit cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality.

Retention

We retain your information for as long as needed to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, to comply with legal or professional obligations, and to resolve disputes.

California and Other Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you may have additional rights under the CCPA or other laws (e.g., access, deletion, opt-out of sale). We do not sell personal information. To exercise applicable rights, contact us using the information below.
